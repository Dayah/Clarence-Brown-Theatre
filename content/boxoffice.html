The Clarence Brown Theatre Box Office sells tickets to almost all performances at the CBT.

They are located in the lobby of the CBT at 1714 Andy Holt Blvd. on the UT Campus
Phone: 865-974-5161 Fax: 865-974-3448

Box Office Hours

Monday through Friday 10:00am to 5:00pm
Open through curtain on performance days
Open 1:00pm through curtain on weekend performance days
