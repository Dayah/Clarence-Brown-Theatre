Romeo & Juliet

The Clarence Brown Theatre will be holding auditions for the University Company production of Romeo & Juliet on Thursday, in the Ula Love Doughty Carousel Theatre, Thursday, November 7 from 7:00 pm to 11:00 pm; Friday, November 8 from 6:00 pm to 10:00 pm and Sunday, November 10 from 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm. Auditions are by appointment only.

Please prepare a one-minute monologue using heightened text.

To schedule an audition or for more information on rehearsal and performance dates and times please contact Betty Tipton, Company Manager at 974-6725.

The Bacchae of Euripides

The Clarence Brown Theatre will be conducting auditions for the fourth production of our 2002-2003 season, The Bacchae of Euripides by Nobel prize-winning author Wole Soyinka. This production is part of UT¹s Africa Semester Celebration.

Please prepare: One minute of verse text (drama or poetry); 16 bars of a song, vocal and/or instrumental; and some movement of any style (dance or other physical forms).

Director is looking for energy, talent, enthusiasm and originality. Ethnic (i.e. African, East Indian, etc.) and contemporary urban forms (i.e. hip hop) are most welcome.
