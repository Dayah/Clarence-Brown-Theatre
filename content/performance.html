There are all kinds of performances happening at the Clarence Brown Theatre. The professional series sponsored by the UT Department of Theatre provides a seven-play season beginning in September and running though June.

The Department of Theatre also sponsors workshops, special performances and hosts guest artists.

In addition, our students are always providing performances for the general public through All Campus Theatre or other student projects performed in the CBT lab theatre.

And if that's not enough entertainment for you, other UT and community organizations use the Clarence Brown Theatre as their performance venue.

Information on any of these events is available by clicking on one of the links below - or by calling the Clarence Brown Theatre Box Office at 865-974-5161.
