As a costume design student at the University of Tennessee, you will have unique opportunities to fulfill your potential as a designer that include opportunities for study and travel in Europe and opportunities to work with some of the finest directors from this country and other countries. You will be challenged to become intellectually, artistically and technically prepared to work in the environment you choose. As a student at the University of Tennessee, you will be educated to design costumes that are dramaturgical extensions of the play, not merely decorative or illustrative elements.
