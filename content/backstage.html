Bring your class or community group for a backstage tour of the Clarence Brown Theatre. Your tour guide will provide you with a historical overview of the CBT and take you on a walking your of the facilities, including the auditorium, stage, scene shop, costume shop, trap room under the stage, and the green room/dressing area. Tours last approximately 45 minutes to an hour.

To schedule a backstage tour, contact the CBT Education/Outreach Director at 974-2485.
