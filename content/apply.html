Requirements

The following must be sent directly to the Graduate School at the above address:

  1. Graduate Application for Admission
  2. $35.00 application fee
  3. Official Copies of Transcripts from ALL schools attended
    (Note: These must be sent directly from the school itself to the Graduate School. Secondary copies will NOT be accepted.)
  4. Foreign Students must take the T.O.E.F.L. and submit their scores.

  1. Three (3) Letters of Recommendation

  3. The student must make arrangements to have their portfolio reviewed by the faculty person in charge of the design area for which they are applying or to audition for the performance program.
