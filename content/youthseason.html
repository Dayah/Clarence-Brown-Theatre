Each year the Clarence Brown Theatre selects three productions for its Season for Youth school-day performances designed to engage young people and expose them to the wonders of live theatre. Discussion guides for teachers are available and a post-performance discussion with the actors is available upon request (reservations are required).

How to Make Reservations

Call (865) 974-8058 to make reservations for Season For youth performances. Please make your reservations as early as possible. All reservations are made on a "first come, first served" basis.

Season for Youth performances frequently sell out and have a waiting list of schools that would like to attend. In order to enable schools to move from the waiting list as space becomes available, payment is required in advance. Please note that any additions to your reservations are subject to ticket availability. If you have any questions about these policies, please call (865) 974-8058.

Children's Theatre Fund

Clarence Brown Theatre supporters have made funds available to enable young people from disadvantaged backgrounds to attend performances at low or no cost. Call (865) 974-8058 to find out more about this opportunity for your students.

Educator's Night at the CBT

Special invitation to all educators: in order to provide teachers with information on specific productions during the CBT season, we are happy to offer all full-time educators the opportunity to buy discounted season tickets for the first Wednesday of the run of each of this season's shows. Your ticket will be for all seven productions. And, as an added bonus, educators will be invited to stay for four post-play discussions led by CBT resident dramaturg, Dr. Klaus van den Berg, and CBT education/outreach director, Kim Midkiff, after the following productions: Nora (adaptation of Ibsen's A Doll's House); The Bacchae of Euripides (adaptation by African playwright Wole Soyinka of the ancient classic); Midwives by Dana Yeaton; and Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet. For more information, call the CBT Box Office at (865) 974-5161 and ask for your educator's discount.
