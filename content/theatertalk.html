Ever wonder why some paintings are considered art and why some are not? What to know how that great scenery gets from a designer's dream onto the stage? Or maybe you can't get enough of those songs from Oliver! Then join us at Barnes & Noble Booksellers for an entertaining and insightful look at each CBT production. Expert guests, cast members, and directors make presentations that spark lively discussions about the current play.

Theatre Talk is held at Barnes & Noble Booksellers on Kingston Pike the Sunday after Opening Night at 2 pm, and is free and open to the public. Drawings will be held for free merchandise.
