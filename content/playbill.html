Tableau

The Tableau is much more than just a playbill for the Clarence Brown Theatre. Articles about the play, the playwright, words from the director and the artistic director of the theatre are included with each issue.

Companies and individuals that support the theatre are also featured in our playbill through their advertisements and our support listings.
